## About Company
Instagram is a social media platform owned by Meta Platforms, Inc., primarily focused on photo and video sharing through its mobile application and website.

## Incident and Exposure
On January 7, 2026, a dataset containing information from 6.2 million Instagram accounts was posted to a hacking forum. This data was allegedly scraped from Instagram's platform using an API. The exposed information includes Instagram display names, usernames, and geographic locations. For a significant portion of these accounts, associated email addresses were also exposed, and some records additionally contained phone numbers.

While the data appears to be publicly available information scraped from profiles, the aggregation of this data, particularly email addresses and phone numbers, increases the risk of targeted phishing attacks. Users may receive unsolicited communications that appear to be from Instagram or related services, attempting to trick them into revealing further personal information or account credentials. There is no indication that account passwords or other sensitive login details were compromised in this incident.

## Next Steps
Given the exposure of email addresses and phone numbers, you should be highly vigilant about unsolicited communications. Be cautious of any emails, text messages, or direct messages that claim to be from Instagram or Meta, especially those asking you to click links, verify account details, or change your password. Always navigate directly to the official Instagram website or app to manage your account settings or respond to any security alerts.

It is recommended to enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your Instagram account if you haven't already. This adds an extra layer of security, requiring a code from your phone in addition to your password when logging in from a new device. Also, review your privacy settings on Instagram to ensure only the information you intend to share publicly is visible. Consider using a unique, strong password for your Instagram account that is not reused on other services.
